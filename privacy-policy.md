Privacy Policy for Basic Music Player

Last updated: 2025/11/25

Basic Music Player (“the App”) is a simple offline music player.
This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the App.

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically, the App does not collect:

Personal information

Contact information

Location data

Identifiers

Device information

Usage data

Audio recordings

Files or media outside of the music files you select on your own device

The App runs entirely offline and performs all features locally on your device.


## 2. No Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

Analytics services

Advertising services

Crash reporting tools

Third-party SDKs

Online tracking technologies

No data is transmitted to external servers.


## 3. How Your Local Files Are Used

The App accesses only the audio files on your device that you choose to play.
These files never leave your device and are never uploaded, shared, or analyzed.


## 4. Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect any personal data from users of any age, including children under 13.


## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If the privacy policy changes, the updated version will be made available through the same link where this policy is hosted.
